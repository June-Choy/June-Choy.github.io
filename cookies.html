Gemma's Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

My favourite type of chocolate chip cookies are crispy on the edges and chewy in the centre. Gemma Stafford's recipes are easy to follow and I love her bakes. I used dark chocolate for the cookies and reduced the amount of sugar.

Recipe

Ingredients Quantity
  • Butter
    		•  240g
  • Light brown sugar
    		•  225g
  • Caster sugar
    		•  100g
  • Plain flour
    		•  355g
  • Salt
    		•  1.5 teaspoon
  • Baking soda
    		•  1 teaspoon
  • Eggs
    		•  2
  • Vanilla extract
    		•  2 teaspoon
  • Dark chocolate chunks
    		•  360g

    Method

    1. Whisk flour, salt and baking soda in a medium bowl.
    2. In a separate bowl, melt butter and add in both brown and white sugar. Whisk vigorously for 1 minute.
    3. Whisk in eggs one at time, until fully combined.
    4. Add vanilla extract.
    5. Add dry ingredients to the wet ingrdients and stir until fully combine.
    6. Stir in chocolate chunks.
    7. Chill for minimum 1 hour.
    8. Shape to desired size.
    9. Bake cookies at 190Â°C for 10 minutes or until golden brown.
    10. Cool on a rack and store in airtight container.
